Enter the dream. Escape the nightmare.

Nightfall Protocol is a cinematic psychological extraction horror game built around tense, unpredictable missions. Enter unstable nightmare zones, complete dangerous objectives, collect rare artifacts, survive adaptive entities, and extract before the Reality Collapse meter reaches zero.

Every run combines randomized objectives, shifting rooms, collapse events, enemy patrols, loot drops, and atmospheric mission lore. Watch the map change under pressure, manage health and sanity, and decide when to risk one more artifact or run for extraction.

Progress through operator levels, build your artifact collection, unlock lore files, and customize your profile with ethical cosmetic-only store items. Purchases never provide gameplay power.

Nightfall Protocol is playable offline and designed for quick solo extraction runs with daily nightmare, endless descent, co-op, and story protocol foundations ready to expand.
